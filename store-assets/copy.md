### Description (4000 char max — preview cuts at ~250 chars before "more…")

```
Titan Protocol is the personal operating system for people who refuse to drift.

Track every domain of your life — body, mind, money, charisma — with the discipline of a system. Tasks. Habits. Goals. Daily scoring. Identity-driven ranks. Now synced across web, desktop, and mobile.

THE FOUR ENGINES
Body. Mind. Money. Charisma. Every action ladders up to one of these. Your daily Titan Score is a weighted average — neglect one and it pulls everything down.

THE DAILY RHYTHM
1. Plan in the morning. A short protocol session sets your intent for the day.
2. Execute. Tick missions as you complete them. Each tap shows up in your engine score within milliseconds.
3. Reflect at night. Journal what worked and what didn't. Tomorrow starts smarter.

YOUR DAILY TITAN SCORE
Every day ends with a number from 0 to 100 — your composite execution score across all four engines. A streak counter rewards consistency. A six-tier rank ladder (Initiate → Operator → Specialist → Vanguard → Sentinel → Titan) rewards depth.

CROSS-DEVICE SYNC
Plan your day on the web in the morning. Tap missions off on your phone during the day. Reflect on the desktop app at night. Every device sees the same data, updated in real time.

WHY TITAN PROTOCOL
Most productivity apps hand you a blank task list and call it a day. Titan Protocol is a system — opinionated, gamified, and built around the idea that your life has structure whether you acknowledge it or not. The four-engine framework forces balance. The daily score forces honesty. The rank ladder rewards the only thing that matters: showing up, again and again.

DATA & PRIVACY
Your data is yours. Synced end-to-end via Supabase with row-level security so only you can read your own rows. Sign out and the device cache wipes itself. Delete your account and everything cascades.

FREE DURING BETA
Pro tier and pricing come later. While we're in beta, every feature is unlocked.

For people who refuse to drift.
```

### What's New in this version

First public release. Multi-device sync, the four-engine framework, daily Titan Score, focus timer, journal, goals, and analytics — all in one personal OS.
